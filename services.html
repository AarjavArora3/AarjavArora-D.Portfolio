Explore my Process

Empathize with my users and stakeholders

Based on new trends and authentic design ideas, my design bandwidth includes creatives such as posters, GIFs, video making & editing, newsletters, advertisements, and much more.

Define the Problem Statement

I synthesize the information I have gathered from the empathize stage and state the problem statement in a clear and concise way. The problem statement describes who I am designing for, what their needs and problems are, and why they are important to solve.

Ideate possible solutions

I brainstorm ideas for possible solutions that can solve the problem statement. I use various brainstorming techniques, such as mind mapping, sketching, etc., to generate as many ideas as possible without judging or filtering them.

Create Prototypes

I create prototypes or drafts of my graphic design solutions. I use tools like Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, XD) to create prototypes of different formats and styles. I make sure that my prototypes communicate my ideas effectively and align with the key messages and tone of voice of the client.

Test my solutions

I test my solutions with users and stakeholders and evaluate their feedback and reactions. I use methods like usability testing, surveys, interviews, etc., to test my prototypes and measure their effectiveness and impact. I validate my assumptions and hypotheses, as well as identify the strengths and weaknesses of my graphic design solutions.

Iterate and improve my solutions

I use the feedback and data from the test stage to iterate and improve my solutions until they meet the user needs and stakeholder goals. I make changes to the content, layout, style, functionality, etc., of my graphic design solutions based on the feedback and data.

Share my process and solutions

I document my process and solutions using storytelling as a format. I create a narrative that explains how I used design thinking, storytelling, and the 4 foundational pillars to solve the problem statement. I share my process and solutions with others using various channels, such as presentations, reports, portfolios, etc.

Reflect on my learnings and outcomes

I reflect on what I learned from this project, what worked well, what didn't work well, what challenges I faced, what opportunities I found, etc. I also reflect on how this project helped me develop my skills as a graphic designer for digital marketing creatives.

Apply my skills and knowledge to new challenges

I seek opportunities to apply my skills and knowledge to new challenges in graphic design for digital marketing creatives. I look for projects that require me to use design thinking, storytelling, and the 4 foundational pillars to solve complex problems creatively and innovatively.
